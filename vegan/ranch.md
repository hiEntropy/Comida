## Name

Vegan Ranch

## Servings

1.75 cups 

## Prep Time 

5 minutes 

## Cook Time 

0

## Ingredience 

* 1½ cup vegan mayo
* ¼ cup non-dairy milk - any unsweetened original flavor
* 1½ teaspoon apple cider vinegar
* 3 cloves garlic - crushed or three tbsp garlic powder
* ½ tablespoon dried parsley
* 1 teaspoon dried dill
* 1 teaspoon onion powder
* ¼ teaspoon paprika
* ¼ teaspoon pepper
* salt - to taste

## Directions

mix it all together
