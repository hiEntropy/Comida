## NAME 
Carolina BBQ Sauce
## Servings 

## Prep Time 

5 minutes

## Cook Time 

10 minutes

## Ingredience

* ¾ cup yellow mustard  
* ½ cup honey  
* ¼ cup brown sugar  
* ½ cup apple cider vinegar  
* 2 tablespoons ketchup  
* 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce  
* 1 teaspoon garlic powder  
* Pinch or more of cayenne  
* ½ teaspoon salt  
* Dash of hot sauce  

## Directions

1. Whisk all of the ingredients together in a large bowl.  
2. Add to a small pot and simmer for 10 minutes. Cool.  
3. Pour into a jar and cover. Refrigerate at least one day to let the flavors mingle.  
4. To serve, warm it up in a small pot and get eating!
