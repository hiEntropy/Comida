## Pork Rib Rub 

## Servings 

Enough for about two racks 

## Prep Time 

15 minutes 

## Cook Time 

0 minutes 

## Ingredience

* 1/3 cup Paprika 
* 1/3 cup Brown Sugar
* 1/4 cup Salt 
* 2 tbs yellow mustard 
* 1 tsp pepper 
* 2 tbs garlic powder 
* 1 tbs coriander 
* 1 tbs red pepper flakes 
* 1 tsp black pepper 
* dried oregano 
 
## Directions

* mix it all and spread it thick on the ribs
